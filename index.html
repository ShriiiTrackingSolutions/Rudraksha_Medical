About

Our center combines advanced technology, skilled specialists, and a patient-first approach to provide reliable treatment across three key areas. Comprehensive Eye Care – Offering solutions for Cataract, LASIK & Refractive Errors, Glaucoma, Retina Disorders, Dry Eye, and more, ensuring clear and healthy vision. NEURO CARE

SEIZURE DISORDERS
Convulsion Care & Diagnosis

Sudden involuntary muscle movements due to seizures need neurological assessment, medication, and trigger management.

LIVER HEALTH
Jaundice Detection & Care

Yellowing of skin or eyes caused by liver dysfunction or infection; requires proper diagnosis and treatment for full recovery.
